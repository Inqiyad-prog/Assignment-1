Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend DevConf?

DevConf is open to everyone, including students, beginners, professional developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts.

Where will the event take place?

The conference will be held at the Grand Convention Center, San Francisco, with online streaming available.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes! All registered attendees will receive access to session recordings after the conference.

Is there a student discount?

Yes. Students can receive a discounted ticket by providing a valid student ID during registration.
